Week 3

MEI has the capacity to encode descriptive data whilst MusicXML does not. There is also a difference between how the two regard certain features. In MusicXML, aspects such as time signatures and pitch (amongst other things) are all defined as elements whilst these same aspects are defined as attributes in MEI. Finally, MusicXML does not register any lyrical additions to the sheet music whilst MEI can read any lyrics added to the score.
